How does the cheese rating system work?

Businesses are rated from 1 to 5 cheese wheels (üßÄ). 1 cheese = not grate, 5 cheese = truly grate! The average of all ratings determines the business's Cheese Score.

Businesses with 4.5+ stars and at least 5 reviews earn our Certified Grateness badge, showing they consistently deliver great service.

Click on any business card, then scroll to the "Grate this service" section. Select your cheese rating, write your experience, and submit!

Currently, reviews are permanent once submitted. Make sure you're happy with your rating before submitting!
